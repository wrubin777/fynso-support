Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
You can sign up using Google, Facebook, or Apple Sign-In. Just open the app and select your preferred sign-in method.
How do subscriptions work?
Fynso offers monthly and yearly premium subscriptions. Subscriptions unlock access to premium workouts and recipes. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's App Store settings.
How do I restore my purchases?
Open the Fynso app, go to the Subscription screen, and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous purchases associated with your Apple ID or Google account.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the Settings screen in the app. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
